üè† Home Tab

The Home tab displays your available word lists (tags) and lets you select what to study.

Selecting a Word List

  1. Browse the available tags/word lists
  2. Tap on a tag to select it
  3. The selected tag will be used for your study session

Understanding Tags

Tags are categories that organize your words. For example:

  • Week 1, Week 2, etc. - Weekly spelling lists
  • Science Words - Subject-specific vocabulary
  • Difficult Words - Words you find challenging

üìù My Words Tab - Manage Your Words

Add your own custom words and organize them with tags.

Adding a New Word

  1. Go to the My Words tab
  2. Tap the "Add Word" button
  3. Enter the spelling word
  4. Enter the Chinese translation (optional)
  5. Select or create a tag/category
  6. Choose Public or Private:
    • Public: Word list is visible to all users
    • Private: Only you can see this word list
  7. Tap "Save" to add the word

Managing Words

  • View Words - See all words in a selected tag
  • Edit Words - Modify word details
  • Delete Words - Remove words you no longer need

Assigning Tags

You can assign existing word lists (tags) to yourself:

  1. Go to More ‚Üí Assign Class
  2. Browse available public tags
  3. Check the tags you want to study
  4. Tap "Save" to assign them to your account

‚ùì Quiz Tab - Test Your Knowledge

Take quizzes to test your spelling skills and track your progress.

Taking a Quiz

  1. Go to the Quiz tab
  2. Select the tag/word list to quiz from
  3. Listen to the word and type your answer
  4. Submit your answer
  5. Continue until the quiz is complete
  6. View your score and results

Quiz Results

After completing a quiz, you'll see:

  • Score - Percentage of correct answers
  • Correct Words - Words you spelled correctly
  • Incorrect Words - Words to practice more
Review your incorrect words after each quiz and practice them in the Study tab!

üí° Tips for Success

1. Practice Regularly

Short, daily practice sessions are more effective than long, occasional ones. Aim for 10-15 minutes each day.

2. Use the Repeat Feature

Set your Spell Repeat Count to 3 or more. Repetition helps commit words to long-term memory.

3. Listen Carefully

Pay attention to pronunciation. Break words into syllables to help remember spelling patterns.

4. Review Mistakes

After each quiz, review the words you got wrong. Create a "Difficult Words" tag for extra practice.

5. Add Your Own Words

When you encounter new words in school or reading, add them to the app right away.

‚ùî Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

Currently, password reset is not available through the app. Please contact support for assistance.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection to sync your data and access word lists.

Q: How do I delete my account?

Go to More ‚Üí My Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.

Q: Can I share my word lists with others?

Yes! When creating a tag, select "Public" to make it visible to all users. They can then assign your word list to their account.

Q: Why can't I access some features?

Some features (My Words, Quiz) require you to be logged in. Create an account or log in to access all features.

Q: How do I change the voice?

Go to More ‚Üí Settings and select your preferred voice from the Voice Selection dropdown.
