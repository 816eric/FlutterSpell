Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your account and all associated data from Spell App.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to delete your account from within the app:

  1. Open the Spell App
  2. Navigate to the "More" tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Tap on "My Account"
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Tap "Delete" to confirm

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Username and password
  • Spelling Words: All words you have added
  • Tags: All tags/categories you have created
  • Quiz History: All quiz attempts and scores
  • Study Progress: Learning history and statistics
  • Settings: All personalized preferences
  • Login History: All login records
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming deletion.

Data Deletion Timeline

Your data will be deleted immediately upon confirmation. The deletion is processed in real-time and is permanent.
