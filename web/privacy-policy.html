Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Welcome to Spell App ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Password (stored securely using encryption)

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use the app:

  • Spelling words you add and practice
  • Quiz scores and learning progress
  • Study session history
  • App settings and preferences

Device Information

We may collect device information such as device type and operating system for app optimization purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the spelling practice service
  • Track your learning progress and statistics
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Improve and optimize our app
  • Respond to your requests or inquiries

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information for analytics purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Text-to-Speech services for pronunciation
  • AI services for word definitions and examples (optional feature)

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be educational and family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app:

  1. Go to the "More" tab
  2. Tap on "My Account"
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

This will permanently delete your account and all associated data including your words, tags, quiz history, and settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
